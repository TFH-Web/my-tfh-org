TFH Kids Ministry Troubleshooting: Check-In

In this section you can find helpful articles for troubleshooting check-in. Just click on a topic to see possible solutions.

    1. Check the database to ensure the children are in the family:

      1. Go to rock.tfh.org & login using your TFH credentials (ie username: first.lastname Password: the same password used for your email)
      2. Go to the search box on the upper right side
      3. Search using the following format: lastname, firstname
        1. If you do not see the family in the search results this means they aren’t in the database; go to the topic “Family Not in Database”
      4. Once you’ve found the family look below the person’s profile picture to where their name and if married their spouse’s name.
        1.  This will be below the badge bar which is where you’ll see icons describing their attendance and involvement. 
      5. If the kids are listed move onto section 2. below
      6. If the children are not in the family, click the pencil icon on the right hand side of the family section.
      7. In Family Members press ‘Add Person’
      8. Fill in the pertinent details and ensure the data is correct.
        1. All children must have a birthday.
        2. If the child is 4yrs old or older they must have a grade as well.
          1. Even if a 4yr old isn’t in Pre-K yet they need to be assigned Pre-K to allow for checkin to assign them to the correct room.
      9. Press ‘Save’ at the bottom of the page, you may have to scroll down
      10. Look the family up on the kiosks and ensure they are able to checkin now


         2. Check the child’s age/grade:

      1. If the kids are listed in the family and they aren’t able to check in you’ll need to check their birthday & grade.
        1. All children must have a birthday.
        2. If the child is 4yrs old or older they must have a grade as well.
          1. Even if a 4yr old isn’t in Pre-K yet they need to be assigned Pre-K to allow for checkin to assign them to the correct room.
      2. If the child is missing either grade or birthday to assign them you’ll need to access the Edit Person page by being on the specific child’s page and then pressing the pencil icon in the top right of the page
        1. Typically its immediately under your name or the search bar
      3. Fill in the birthday and/or grade fields
      4. Select save in the bottom left of the page, you may need to scroll down a bit
      5. Look the family up on the kiosks and ensure they are able to checkin now

  •     a. No light on the printer:

                i. Check to make sure printer is plugged in.

                ii. Make sure switch on back is turned on.    

                iii. If neither of these work contact support at: support@tfh.org

        b. Light is blinking green:

                 i. Quickly press the “feed” button on the top of the printer.

        c. Light is blinking red:

                 i. Check the lid and ensure it is fully shut.
                 ii. Check for either a jam or a sticker getting stuck on a roller.

        d. Light is solid green, but it’s not printing:

                 i. Check if the USB cables are plugged in and fully seated in both the computer and printer.
                 ii. Make sure you’ve selected the correct printer in the setup menu. To check this:
                     1. Close the checkin app, swipe from left to right at the edge of the screen and press the “X”
                     2. Restart the Rock Checkin app, tap on the orange app on the desktop.
                     3. Check what printer was selected. It should be a “ZDesigner …..” printer.

        e. If you are still having issues please contact support at: support@tfh.org
