TFH Kids Ministry Check-In Setup

In this section you can find helpful articles for setting up check-in. Just click on a topic for help.

  1. Power on the kiosks by pressing the power button on the right side of the computer.
    1. For The Passage the power button will be on the top just behind the front bevel.
  2. When prompted input the PIN number: 1324
    1. For The Passage the PIN is: 1234
  3. Make sure the printers are on.
    1. If there isn’t a light on the top of them, their power switch is on the back.

  1. As the computer turns on it should open the Rock checkin app.
    1. It may take a minute or two after windows has fully booted up, if it doesn’t double tap on the app.
  2. Once the checkin app is open make sure the checkin addres is correct: http://rock.tfh.org/checkin 
    1. If after you press the OK button it asks you for a username and passcode (it should store them, but if you have to type it make sure you hit the "remember me on this computer") use the following credentials:
      1. Username: checkin
      2. Password: RockCheckin
  3. Check the printer selection is correct the ZDesigner should be selected in orange.
  4. Press OK in the bottom left corner.
  5. Select the appropriate theme
    1. RV/EB/Napa: 01-TFHKids-General
    2. The Passage: 02-TFHKids-ThePassage
    3. The Landing: 03-TFHKids-TheLanding
  6. Select your Kiosk Device
    1. East Bay: EB Kiosk
    2. Napa: NP Kiosk
    3. Roseville: RV Kiosk
    4. Vacaville (Both The Passage & The Landing): VV B & C Kiosks
  7. Select your Check-in Areas:
    1. RV/EB/Napa: 
      1. Nursery
      2. Elementary
      3. Preteen
      4. Preschool
    2. Vacaville
      1. Nursery
      2. Elementary
      3. Preteen
      4. Preschool
      5. Champions (This will be listed in the right side under ‘Additional Area(s)’
  8. You should be good to go. If you run into any issues please go to: https://my.tfh.org/resources/checkin-kidsmin/troubleshoot
