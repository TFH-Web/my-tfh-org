Our vision is to see people thriving in life-changing relationships where they can discover the full life God has intended, and receive the encouragement to walk it out.




A TFH core value: The church isn’t a building, it’s a community. We find our significance through relationship.  Groups come in all shapes and sizes. No matter where you are in life, we’re positive you’ll find people with similar interests, ready to connect and do life with you.

It’s our desire to make it easy for men to pursue authentic community, in a safe group contexts, to grow spiritually and achieve personal changes faster than we ever could on our own.

TFH Women is a growing community of women of all ages choosing to live life and its seasons TOGETHER and not alone.

Our vision is to see couples discover their identity entering into marriage, learn flexibility for married life, increase generosity as they grow their families, and build a legacy to pass on to the next generation.

Our vision for Encounter groups is for students to grow and make friends.

TFH Common groups exist to provide a growing community that encourages individuals to know Christ, follow Him, enjoy the journey and discover significance through relationships.
