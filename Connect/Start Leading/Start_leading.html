Leadership is influence. Some allow the perception of being “unqualified” keep them from stepping into a leadership role, but if you have influence, you’re already leading.

We invite you to start the process of becoming a leader in TFH Groups and the Artist Collective if you can do these things: encourage people, listen to their stories, and pray for them.

Attend the GROW class. Week four is a TFH Group Leader training.

Complete and meet the requirements of the TFH Group Leader Application.
